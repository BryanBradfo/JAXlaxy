## 🧭 Why Navigate with JAXlaxy?

The JAX landscape moves fast. Libraries emerge, evolve, and sometimes fade into the background. This repository helps you:

  * **Find the Right Tool for the Job:** Whether you are building LLMs, Robotics controllers, or Bio-informatic pipelines.

## 🗺️ The Galactic Map

To help you find your way, we’ve categorized the ecosystem into stellar systems:

### ☀️ The Sun: Core JAX & Compilers

  * **[JAX](https://github.com/google/jax)**: The heart of the system. Autograd and XLA.
  * **[Pallas](https://jax.readthedocs.io/en/latest/pallas/index.html)**: For writing custom, high-performance GPU/TPU kernels.
  * **[XLA](https://github.com/openxla/xla)**: The compiler making everything run at light speed.

### 🪐 The Giants: High-Level Frameworks

  * **[Flax (NNX)](https://github.com/google/flax)**: The current Google standard. Flexible, powerful, and scalable.
  * **[Equinox](https://github.com/patrick-kidger/equinox)**: Pure elegance. Everything is a PyTree. Highly recommended for those coming from PyTorch.
  * **[MaxText](https://github.com/google/maxtext)**: Google's flagship for training LLMs at massive scale.

### 🛰️ The Satellites: Crucial Utilities

  * **[Optax](https://github.com/google-deepmind/optax)**: Gradient processing and optimization, period.
  * **[Orbax](https://github.com/google/orbax)**: The modern solution for checkpointing and model exporting.
  * **[Grain](https://github.com/google/grain)**: The new standard for high-speed, multi-host data loading.

## ⚠️ Legacy Radar (Warning Zone)

*Innovation moves fast. These tools were pioneers but are generally not recommended for new projects in 2026:*

  * **Trax**: 🛑 Obsolete. Use Flax or MaxText instead.
  * **Haiku**: 🟡 Stable but in maintenance mode. DeepMind is largely pivoting toward Flax NNX for new research.
  * **Objax**: 🔴 Low activity.
